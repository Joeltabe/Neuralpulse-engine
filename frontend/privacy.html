Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 29, 2026

1. Information We Collect

When you use NeuralPulse Engine, we collect the following information:

  • Account Information: Email address, name, and password hash when you register.
  • Usage Data: Uploaded ad creative files, analysis results, and interaction history.
  • Payment Information: Payment processing is handled by Stripe. We do not store credit card details on our servers.
  • Technical Data: Browser type, device information, IP address, and usage patterns for service improvement.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use collected data to:

  • Provide and maintain the neural analysis service
  • Process token purchases and manage your account
  • Improve our AI models and analysis accuracy
  • Send service-related communications (receipts, updates)
  • Detect and prevent abuse of our platform

3. Data Storage & Security

All data is stored on secure servers using industry-standard encryption. We use:

  • Neon PostgreSQL with serverless pooling for database storage
  • Encrypted connections (SSL/TLS) for all data in transit
  • Password hashing via bcrypt for credential security
  • JWT tokens for session management

4. Data Retention

We retain your analysis history and account data for as long as your account is active. You may request deletion of your data at any time by contacting support. Uploaded files are deleted immediately after analysis.

5. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Stripe: Payment processing (token purchases)
  • Neon: PostgreSQL database hosting
  • Chart.js: Client-side data visualization

6. Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal data. To exercise these rights, contact us at privacy@neuralpulse.ai.
